About the Farm

Rock walls, perennial gardens, tranquil rolling fields provide the pastoral setting, which hosts the bright year round living space known as Locke Falls Farm. The 3600sq’/5 bedroom home offers ample bedding for your bridal party and the sprawling layout allows for a considerable number of guests to enjoy both indoor and outdoor entertaining.

Prep pantry with commercial fridge/freezer, large open working kitchen with 6 burner Viking gas stove and double wall ovens lead to several wooden decks, with an over-sized outdoor gas grill. An expansive quartzite patio with ample furniture and outside fire pit surrounds the deck areas above the 36’X36’ Barn. The property offers multiple ceremony locations and level grassy settings for portable tent structures depending on your event needs.
