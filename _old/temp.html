About the Farm

Rock walls, perennial gardens, tranquil rolling fields provide the pastoral setting, which hosts the bright year round living space and event barn known as Locke Falls Farm. The 3600sq’/5 bedroom home and barn will offer ample bedding for your bridal party and a sprawling layout for your guests to enjoy both indoor and outdoor entertaining.
