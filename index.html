About the Farm

Rock walls, perennial gardens, tranquil rolling fields provide the pastoral setting, which hosts the bright year round living space known as Locke Falls Farm. The 3600sq’/5 bedroom home offers ample bedding for your bridal party and the sprawling layout allows for a considerable number of guests to enjoy both indoor and outdoor entertaining.

Our History

Wonalancet translates to ‘Pleasant Breathing’… And what better way to celebrate your special event than taking in the sweet tranquility of Locke Falls Farm. The home was built in 1844 with existing hand hewn beams, later ran as Locke Falls Cottage Inn where it was known for the picturesque and extensive grounds, proximity to mountain climbing and summer rambles during the early 1900’s.

When you consider the Farm’s rich history, the beauty of New Hampshire, pair that with the flexibility  to create your own elegant country experience and you finish with a spectacular wedding weekend full of beautiful memories.
